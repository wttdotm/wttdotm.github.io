Animal Escapes

Smithsonian National Zoological Park investigations, 2014–2021

This released Smithsonian record, published by GovernmentAttic, lists animal escape and containment incidents at the National Zoo between 2014 and 2021. It includes dates, species, circumstances, response language, search procedures, recovery outcomes, and small details that make an institution's effort to control animal movement unexpectedly visible.
