iNaturalist

Community observations of living things

iNaturalist is a global platform where people upload encounters with plants, animals, fungi, and other organisms. An observation can include a photo or sound, a date, a place, an identification, and a conversation in which other users confirm, refine, or disagree with what was seen.

Dataset-Specific Instructions

You can search the dataset by species, location, date, and user. Play around with the filters and different views!

Questions to ask

  • Are these records of more than just animals and plants? What else is this the product of?
  • Can you find your favorite animal? What do you see?
  • Do different people have different interests? How does this affect the dataset?
  • What other views of this data did you wish you had?
