Fancams

Panoramic crowd photography from public events

Fancam makes ultra-wide crowd images at sports and entertainment events. The images can be explored at a scale where a stadium is both a single scene and a collection of thousands of small, accidental portraits. Its directory spans events hosted by teams, leagues, venues, and organizations around the world.

The image is built for the spectator who wants to find themself. But it also records everyone else: people cheering, looking away, checking a phone, arriving late, or becoming briefly visible in a crowd whose main event is somewhere outside the frame.
