Bluesky Firehose

Live public social-media activity

Bluesky's public AT Protocol makes its stream of new posts and replies available in real time. Firesky is a viewer that republishes this flow as it happens: a succession of ordinary updates, reactions, links, jokes, fragments of news, and conversations across languages. It can be filtered by keywords, account, linked domain, language, and post type.

Questions to ask

  • What are you surprised to see in these posts?
  • What does speed and scale of the deluge remind you of?
  • How would you slow this down or make it faster?
